Michigan Ballot Preview

Ballot Preview is a service to view sample ballots for elections in Michigan, annotate them, and share how you plan to vote with your friends and familiy on social media.

This is accomplished by looking up your voter registration using the Michigan Elections API to find your precinct and rendering a sample ballot.

At no point do we store information about your voting preferences. All ballot selections become part of the URL, so only people you share your annotated ballot with will know how you plan to vote.
